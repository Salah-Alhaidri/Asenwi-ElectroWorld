How to Complete Your Order

Unfortunately, electronic payment options are not available in Yemen at this time. To complete your order, please follow these steps:

  1. Take a Screenshot:
    Take a screenshot of this page (including the order summary above) by pressing Print Screen on your keyboard or using your device's screenshot feature.
  2. Send the Screenshot:
    Send the screenshot to the store owner via WhatsApp or Email. Include your contact details (name, phone number, and address) so we can process your order.
  3. Wait for Confirmation:
    Once the store owner receives your screenshot, they will confirm your order and provide further instructions for payment and delivery.
